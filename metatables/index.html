Module functions.metatables

gfs-metatables

These are tools for preprocessing inside the technology stack of gfs-zürich. The Metatable2 allows you to do SPSS-like preprocessing in Python. It introduces the concept of a group. A group is basically a group of columns that should be preprocessed together.
